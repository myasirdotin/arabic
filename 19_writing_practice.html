📌 Arabic Writing Practice (التعبير الكتابي)

تمارين في الكتابة العربية — Structured Composition Exercises

Develop your Arabic writing skills through guided exercises that apply grammar rules in context.

✅ Writing Exercise 1: Daily Routine

📝 Prompt:

Describe your daily routine using at least 5 different verbs in the present tense. Include time markers like:

  • في الصباح (in the morning)
  • بعد الظهر (in the afternoon)
  • قبل النوم (before sleep)

✓ Checklist:

  • All verbs agree with subject in gender/number
  • Time expressions are properly connected
  • At least one Form II verb (e.g., أُحَضِّرُ)
  • Two adjectives describing activities

في الصباح، أَسْتَيْقِظُ مُبَكِّراً وَأُصَلِّي الفَجْرَ. بَعْدَ ذَلِكَ، أَتَناوَلُ فَطُوراً صِحِّياً وَأَذْهَبُ إِلَى العَمَلِ. في الظُّهْرِ، آكُلُ غَدَاءً مَع زُمَلَائِي. أَعُودُ إِلَى البَيْتِ حَوَالَيْ السَّاعَةِ الخَامِسَةِ وَأُحَضِّرُ العَشَاءَ. قَبْلَ النَّوْمِ، أَقْرَأُ كِتَاباً أَو أَسْتَمِعُ إِلَى مُوسِيقَى.

Translation: In the morning, I wake up early and pray Fajr. After that, I eat a healthy breakfast and go to work. At noon, I eat lunch with my colleagues. I return home around 5 o'clock and prepare dinner. Before sleep, I read a book or listen to music.

🎯 Writing Exercise 2: Past Event

📝 Prompt:

Write about a memorable trip using past tense verbs. Include:

  • 3+ conjugated past verbs
  • One conditional sentence (لَوْ... لَ...)
  • Two descriptive adjectives
  • One relative clause (الَّذِي/الَّتِي)

ذَهَبْتُ إِلَى البَحْرِ العَامَ المَاضِي مَعَ عَائِلَتِي. زُرْنَا المَدِينَةَ التَّارِيخِيَّةَ الَّتِي كَانَتْ قَرِيبَةً مِنَ الفُنْدُقِ. لَوْ نَزَلْنَا المَطَرُ لَمَا تَمَكَّنَّا مِنَ الزِّيَارَةِ، لَكِنَّ الجَوَّ كَانَ مُشْمِساً. أَكَلْنَا طَعَاماً لَذِيذاً فِي مَطْعَمٍ عَلَى الشَّاطِئِ. اِشْتَرَيْتُ تِذْكَارَاتٍ جَمِيلَةً لأَصْدِقَائِي.

Translation: I went to the beach last year with my family. We visited the historic city that was near the hotel. If it had rained, we wouldn't have been able to visit, but the weather was sunny. We ate delicious food at a restaurant on the shore. I bought beautiful souvenirs for my friends.

📘 Writing Exercise 3: Opinion Paragraph

📝 Prompt:

Express your opinion about technology in education using:

  • Present tense for general truths
  • One Form IV verb (e.g., أَسْهَمَتْ - contributed)
  • Connectors (لِأَنَّ، بِالإضَافَةِ إِلَى، مَعَ ذَلِكَ)
  • At least one passive verb

أَعْتَقِدُ أَنَّ التِّكْنُولُوجْيَا أَسْهَمَتْ فِي تَطْوِيرِ التَّعْلِيمِ. بِالإضَافَةِ إِلَى ذَلِكَ، يُسْتَخْدَمُ الحاسوبُ فِي كَثِيرٍ مِنَ الفُصُولِ الدِّراسِيَّةِ لِأَنَّهُ يُسَهِّلُ العَمَلِيَّةَ التَّعْلِيمِيَّةَ. مَعَ ذَلِكَ، لا يَنْبَغِي أَنْ نَعْتَمِدَ عَلَيْهَا كُلِّيَّاً.

Translation: I believe technology has contributed to developing education. Additionally, computers are used in many classrooms because they facilitate the educational process. However, we shouldn't depend on them completely.

📝 Writing Checklist:

Before Submitting Any Writing:

  1. Check all verbs agree with their subjects
  2. Verify case endings on nouns
  3. Ensure definite/indefinite markers are correct
  4. Use at least one complex sentence structure
  5. Include vocabulary from recent lessons
