📿 Arabic Writing Practice (التعبير الكتابي)

تمارين في الكتابة العربية — Structured Composition Exercises

Develop your Arabic writing skills through guided exercises that apply grammar rules in context.

🕌 Writing Exercise 1: Islamic Story

📝 Prompt:

Write a short paragraph about the Prophet's Hijrah (migration to Medina) using:

  • 5+ past tense verbs
  • One relative clause (الَّذِي/الَّتِي)
  • Two descriptive adjectives
  • Time expressions (قَبْلَ، بَعْدَ، فِي)

✓ Checklist:

  • All verbs are properly conjugated in past tense
  • Includes at least one name of a companion
  • Uses proper nouns like مَكَّةَ، المَدِينَةَ
  • Contains one compound sentence

هَاجَرَ الرَّسُولُ صَلَّى اللهُ عَلَيْهِ وَسَلَّمَ مِنْ مَكَّةَ إِلَى المَدِينَةِ مَعَ صَدِيقِهِ أَبِي بَكْرٍ الصِّدِّيقِ. تَرَكُوا مَكَّةَ لَيْلًا وَأَخْفَى اللهُ آثَارَهُمَا. نَزَلُوا فِي غَارِ ثَوْرٍ الَّذِي كَانَ أَمِينًا. بَعْدَ ثَلَاثَةِ أَيَّامٍ، وَاصَلُوا رِحْلَتَهُمَا إِلَى المَدِينَةِ حَيْثُ اسْتَقْبَلَهُمُ الأَنْصَارُ بِفَرَحٍ عَظِيمٍ.

Translation: The Prophet (peace be upon him) migrated from Makkah to Madinah with his companion Abu Bakr As-Siddiq. They left Makkah at night and Allah concealed their traces. They stayed in the cave of Thawr which was safe. After three days, they continued their journey to Madinah where the Ansar welcomed them with great joy.

🎯 Writing Exercise 2: Daily Routine

📝 Prompt:

Describe your daily routine using at least 5 different verbs in the present tense. Include time markers like:

  • في الصباح (in the morning)
  • بعد الظهر (in the afternoon)
  • قبل النوم (before sleep)

✓ Checklist:

  • All verbs agree with subject in gender/number
  • Time expressions are properly connected
  • At least one Form II verb (e.g., أُحَضِّرُ)
  • Two adjectives describing activities

في الصباح، أَسْتَيْقِظُ مُبَكِّراً وَأُصَلِّي الفَجْرَ. بَعْدَ ذَلِكَ، أَتَناوَلُ فَطُوراً صِحِّياً وَأَذْهَبُ إِلَى العَمَلِ. في الظُّهْرِ، آكُلُ غَدَاءً مَع زُمَلَائِي. أَعُودُ إِلَى البَيْتِ حَوَالَيْ السَّاعَةِ الخَامِسَةِ وَأُحَضِّرُ العَشَاءَ. قَبْلَ النَّوْمِ، أَقْرَأُ كِتَاباً

Translation: In the morning, I wake up early and pray Fajr. After that, I eat a healthy breakfast and go to work. At noon, I eat lunch with my colleagues. I return home around 5 o'clock and prepare dinner. Before sleep, I read a book.

📘 Writing Exercise 3: Opinion Paragraph

📝 Prompt:

Express your opinion about technology in education using:

  • Present tense for general truths
  • One Form IV verb (e.g., أَسْهَمَتْ - contributed)
  • Connectors (لِأَنَّ، بِالإضَافَةِ إِلَى، مَعَ ذَلِكَ)
  • At least one passive verb

أَعْتَقِدُ أَنَّ التِّكْنُولُوجْيَا أَسْهَمَتْ فِي تَطْوِيرِ التَّعْلِيمِ. بِالإضَافَةِ إِلَى ذَلِكَ، يُسْتَخْدَمُ الحاسوبُ فِي كَثِيرٍ مِنَ الفُصُولِ الدِّراسِيَّةِ لِأَنَّهُ يُسَهِّلُ العَمَلِيَّةَ التَّعْلِيمِيَّةَ. مَعَ ذَلِكَ، لا يَنْبَغِي أَنْ نَعْتَمِدَ عَلَيْهَا كُلِّيَّاً.

Translation: I believe technology has contributed to developing education. Additionally, computers are used in many classrooms because they facilitate the educational process. However, we shouldn't depend on them completely.

📝 Writing Checklist:

Before Submitting Any Writing:

  1. Check all verbs agree with their subjects
  2. Verify case endings on nouns
  3. Ensure definite/indefinite markers are correct
  4. Use at least one complex sentence structure
  5. Include vocabulary from recent lessons
