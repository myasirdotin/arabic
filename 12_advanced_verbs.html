📌 Advanced Verb Conjugation

الفعل الماضي والمضارع والأمر — Mastering Verb Forms

Now that you know verb types, let's dive deeper into conjugation patterns for different tenses and subjects.

✅ Complete Conjugation Tables:

Past Tense (الماضي) - فعل "كَتَبَ" (to write)

Person Singular Dual Plural
3rd masc. كَتَبَ كَتَبَا كَتَبُوا
3rd fem. كَتَبَتْ كَتَبَتَا كَتَبْنَ
2nd masc. كَتَبْتَ كَتَبْتُمَا كَتَبْتُمْ
2nd fem. كَتَبْتِ كَتَبْتُمَا كَتَبْتُنَّ
1st كَتَبْتُ كَتَبْنَا كَتَبْنَا

Present Tense (المضارع) - فعل "يَكْتُبُ" (to write)

Person Singular Dual Plural
3rd masc. يَكْتُبُ يَكْتُبَانِ يَكْتُبُونَ
3rd fem. تَكْتُبُ تَكْتُبَانِ يَكْتُبْنَ
2nd masc. تَكْتُبُ تَكْتُبَانِ تَكْتُبُونَ
2nd fem. تَكْتُبِينَ تَكْتُبَانِ تَكْتُبْنَ
1st أَكْتُبُ نَكْتُبُ نَكْتُبُ

🎯 Subjunctive & Jussive Moods:

Subjunctive (منصوب)

After particles like لِـ (to) or أَنْ (that):

أُرِيدُ أَنْ أَكْتُبَ
I want to write (ends with فتحة)

Jussive (مجزوم)

After particles like لَمْ (did not):

لَمْ يَكْتُبْ
He did not write (ends with سكون)

📘 Doubled Verbs (المضعف):

Verbs with identical second and third radicals (e.g., مَدَّ "to extend"):

  • Past: مَدَّ (madda)
  • Present: يَمُدُّ (yamuddu)
  • Rule: Second radical doubles in present tense

✍️ Conjugation Practice:

Conjugate these in past tense:

  1. They (fem.) read → قَرَأْنَ
  2. You (dual) went → ذَهَبْتُمَا
  3. We understood → فَهِمْنَا
