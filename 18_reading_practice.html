Apply your grammar knowledge to real Arabic texts with these guided reading exercises.

✅ Reading Passage 1: Short Story

ذَهَبَ عُمَرُ إِلَى السُّوقِ يَوْمَ الجُمُعَةِ. رَأَى فِيهِ كَثِيراً مِنَ النَّاسِ وَالسِّلَعِ. اِشْتَرَى خُبْزاً وَفَاكِهَةً وَزَجَاجَةَ حَلِيبٍ. ثُمَّ لَقِيَ صَدِيقَهُ خَالِداً فَتَحَدَّثَا قَلِيلاً. عِنْدَمَا وَصَلَ إِلَى البَيْتِ، وَجَدَ أَنَّ الحَلِيبَ قَدْ سَكَبَ فِي الحَقِيبَةِ!

📖 New Vocabulary

سِلَع Goods/merchandise
زَجَاجَة Bottle
سَكَبَ Spilled
لَقِيَ Met (past of يلقى)

🔍 Grammar Analysis

Structure Example Explanation
Past Tense Narrative ذَهَبَ، رَأَى، اِشْتَرَى All verbs in past tense for storytelling
Idaafa Construction زَجَاجَةَ حَلِيبٍ Milk bottle (genitive case)
Temporal Clause عِنْدَمَا وَصَلَ "When he arrived" (time marker)
▶ Show Translation

Omar went to the market on Friday. He saw many people and goods there. He bought bread, fruit, and a bottle of milk. Then he met his friend Khalid and they talked a little. When he arrived home, he found that the milk had spilled in the bag!

🎯 Reading Passage 2: News Excerpt

أَعْلَنَتِ الحُكُومَةُ أَنَّهَا سَتُطَوِّرُ المَطَارَ الجَدِيدَ فِي أَشْهُرٍ قَلِيلَةٍ. قَالَ وَزِيرُ النَّقْلِ: "سَيَكُونُ هَذَا المَطَارُ الأَكْبَرَ فِي البِلَادِ". يُتَوَقَّعُ أَنْ يَسْتَقْبِلَ المَطَارُ أَكْثَرَ مِنْ خَمْسَةِ مَلَايِينَ مُسَافِرٍ كُلَّ سَنَةٍ.

📖 New Vocabulary

أَعْلَنَتْ Announced (fem. verb)
سَتُطَوِّرُ Will develop (Form II future)
يُتَوَقَّعُ It is expected (passive)

✍️ Comprehension Questions:

  1. What will the government develop? المطار الجديد (The new airport)
  2. How many passengers are expected? أكثر من خمسة ملايين (More than five million)
  3. Find a Form II verb in the text: سَتُطَوِّرُ (will develop)

📘 Reading Tips:

1. Decoding Strategies

  • Identify verbs first (look for prefixes/suffixes)
  • Spot definite articles (الـ) to find nouns
  • Look for common connectors (و، ف، ثم)

2. Context Clues

Use surrounding words to guess unfamiliar vocabulary:

اشترى خبزاً وفاكهةً → Even if you don't know فاكهة, خبز (bread) suggests it's food.
