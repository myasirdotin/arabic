اختبار شامل — Comprehensive Assessment of Lessons 1-19

This final test evaluates your understanding of all grammar concepts covered in the course. Try to complete it without referring back to lessons.

🖊️ Section 1: Nouns & Sentence Structure

1. Identify the case ending for the underlined word:
رَأَيْتُ الطَّالِبَ (I saw the student)

Answer: فتحة (accusative case)

2. Convert to dual form:
كِتَابٌ جَدِيدٌ (a new book)

Answer: كِتَابَانِ جَدِيدَانِ

🔤 Section 2: Verbs & Conjugation

3. Conjugate in present tense (he/she):
كَتَبَ (to write)

Answer: يَكْتُبُ

4. Convert to Form II with translation:
فَهِمَ (to understand)

Answer: فَهَّمَ (to make understand/explain)

🕌 Section 3: Islamic Arabic Application

5. Analyze the grammar in this Islamic phrase:
إِنَّ الصَّلَاةَ كَانَتْ عَلَى الْمُؤْمِنِينَ كِتَابًا مَوْقُوتًا

Identify:

  • The grammatical case of الصَّلَاةَ
  • The verb form of كَانَتْ
  • The type of مَوْقُوتًا
Answer:
  • الصَّلَاةَ - منصوب (accusative) because of إِنَّ
  • كَانَتْ - past tense, feminine singular
  • مَوْقُوتًا - adjective describing كِتَابًا, منصوب

6. Write a sentence about Ramadan using:

  • One Form IV verb
  • A relative clause
  • A time expression
Answer (sample): في رَمَضَانَ، أُحِبُّ القُرْآنَ الَّذِي أَنْزَلَهُ اللهُ فِي هَذَا الشَّهْرِ
(In Ramadan, I love the Quran which Allah revealed in this month)
